# HR & Labor Management Skill

## Purpose
Manage hiring, payroll, compliance, seasonal workers, safety protocols, and training for ranch operations.

## Labor Categories

| Category | Typical Count | Season | Key Regulations |
|----------|-------------|--------|-----------------|
| Full-time ranch hands | 2-4 | Year-round | FLSA, OSHA, state labor |
| Seasonal haying crew | 2-4 | May-August | H-2A (if foreign), FLSA overtime exemptions |
| Calving season help | 1-2 | Feb-April | Same as above |
| Veterinary (contracted) | 1 (on call) | As needed | State veterinary license |
| Custom operators | As needed | Haying, harvesting | 1099 independent contractor rules |

## Compliance Checklist

### Federal
- [ ] I-9 verification for all employees (within 3 days of hire)
- [ ] W-4 and state withholding forms
- [ ] FLSA poster displayed
- [ ] OSHA Form 300 log (if >10 employees)
- [ ] Workers' compensation insurance
- [ ] H-2A program compliance (if using foreign seasonal workers)
  - [ ] Housing inspection
  - [ ] Transportation guarantee
  - [ ] 3/4 guarantee (minimum hours)
  - [ ] Adverse effect wage rate (AEWR)

### State (varies — check local)
- [ ] State workers' comp
- [ ] State unemployment insurance registration
- [ ] Agricultural labor exemptions (often exempt from overtime)
- [ ] Ranch/farm vehicle insurance

## Safety Protocols

### Daily Briefing Points
- Weather conditions affecting safety
- Equipment operation reminders
- Chemical handling (if applying herbicide/pesticide)
- Livestock handling safety (bull behavior, calving assistance)

### Incident Response
1. **Medical emergency**: Call 911, then notify ranch manager
2. **Equipment accident**: Shut down, secure scene, document
3. **Chemical exposure**: MSDS sheets on-site, rinse protocol, seek medical
4. **Animal injury**: Isolate animal, assess, call vet if needed

### Required Training
- Equipment operation (annual refresher)
- Chemical handling (seasonal, before application)
- Livestock handling (new hires + annual)
- Heat stress awareness (spring briefing)
- Cold weather protocols (fall briefing)

## Payroll & Scheduling

### Pay Periods
- **Full-time**: Bi-weekly, direct deposit
- **Seasonal**: Weekly, check or direct deposit
- **Contracted**: Per job, 1099 at year-end

### Record Keeping
- Daily time sheets (required for H-2A)
- Task assignments and completion
- Safety briefing attendance
- Equipment operation logs
